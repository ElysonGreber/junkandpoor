How to Play

  1. Receive ETH on the Sepolia network by visiting the links.
  2. Connect your wallet to view your balance.
  3. Get 1 JKP using the button or via the Uniswap link.
  4. Place your 1 JKP bet — first, you need to approve the token transfer. Wait for the transaction to complete. Then, a second request will appear in your wallet. Confirm the bet payment. Once the transaction is finalized, you can make your move.
  5. Choose your move and wait for the result. If you win, another transaction will be triggered and the prize will be sent to your wallet.
  6. Your tokens can be swapped for ETH (Sepolia Testnet) or vice versa via Uniswap.
  7. Your game history can be checked after connecting your wallet by clicking on "Played Games History".
  8. Your progress is tracked by the number of victories. Upon reaching 10 wins, the player becomes eligible to mint the Early Adopter NFT. Upon reaching 30 wins, they become eligible to mint the OG Hands NFT.
  9. Have fun!
